A great part of Ghanaian culture is their naming system. It is a highly intricate system that assigns different names to children based on their gender. Names are assigned based on the day of the week in which the children were born.

Using your date of birth that you will enter in the form on the next page, we will be able to apply a complex calculation which will determine which day of the week you were born and hence assign your honorary Akan name.
